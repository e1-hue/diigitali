Category 2A Inspection
Clean the exterior of the CRTi tool.
Inspect casing seal assembly. Replace seal assembly as required.
Assess latching alignment marks and repaint if necessary.
Unlatch CRT and remove a die; evaluate thread compound condition and coverage.
Check all exposed fasteners for signs of damage.
Clean tool joint thread and conduct visual inspection to ensure that condition is acceptable.
Check that the outside diameter of the Casing Seal Assembly is equal to or greater than the maximum inside diameter of the pipe that is being run.
Reassemble the CRTi tool: Reapply appropriate lubricants, re-torque fasteners, and replace seals and fasteners as needed.
Category 2B Inspection
Perform all the steps above.
Visually inspect tooth wear on dies.
Inspect thread compound on cam surfaces to ensure adequate coverage and no contamination.
Clean thread compound from Lower Cam and Intermediate Cam. Conduct a visual inspection of the cams, Active Foot, and Latch.
Examine the sealing surface on the Mandrel below the Upper Cam.
Examine the Upper Cam.
Examine the Mandrel and Jaw cones.
Reassemble the CRTi tool: Reapply appropriate lubricants, re-torque fasteners, and replace seals and fasteners as needed.
